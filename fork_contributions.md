### [`Project/Spain.ipynb`](https://github.com/RakeshSwain2604/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/Project/Spain.ipynb)
**Ebro Delta Coastal Erosion Analysis (2016–2024)**

Tracks coastline change on the Ebro Delta (Tarragona, Spain) using Sentinel-2 imagery from Microsoft Planetary Computer and Copernicus Marine environmental variables. Searches for low-cloud-cover scenes across 5 years (2016–2024), loads RGB + NIR bands via `odc.stac`, and computes coastal change metrics with `hvplot` visualizations.

**Copernicus Marine Wave Model Access**

Demonstrates accessing IBI wave climatology (`cmems_mod_ibi_wav_my_0.027deg-climatology_P1M-m`) via `copernicusmarine.open_dataset`. Selects significant wave height (`VHM0_mean`) over a multi-year time range and creates interactive map visualizations with `hvplot` and an OSM tile background.

### [`Project/planetary_computer_2.ipynb`](https://github.com/RakeshSwain2604/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/Project/planetary_computer_2.ipynb)
**Cloud-Native Satellite Data Explorer (Panel App)**

A Panel-based interactive app (noted as generated by Gemini Pro 2.5) that loads Sentinel-2 RGB imagery over Cape Cod from Planetary Computer via `pystac_client` and `odc.stac`. Uses `pn.cache` to avoid reloading data on each interaction and displays true-color imagery with a date dropdown for scene selection.

### [`Project/project_Alacranes_reef_island.ipynb`](https://github.com/RakeshSwain2604/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/Project/project_Alacranes_reef_island.ipynb)
**Alacranes Reef Island Change Analysis**

Analyzes the Alacranes Reef (Parque Nacional Arrecife Alacranes, Gulf of Mexico) across 5 years (2016–2024) using Sentinel-2 imagery from Planetary Computer. Searches for dry-season and pre-hurricane season scenes, verifies the bounding box with an `hvplot`/`geopandas` basemap, and computes vegetation/water indices over time.

### [`taranto-icechunk-append-rswain.ipynb`](https://github.com/RakeshSwain2604/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/taranto-icechunk-append-rswain.ipynb)
**Taranto SHYFEM → Icechunk Append (rswain bucket)**

Personalized version of the Taranto Icechunk append workflow, writing to the `rswain` S3 bucket. Uses set-difference logic (`set_cloud - set_repo`) to append only new dates to the virtual Icechunk store. ## [maexer1998/MAGICA-School-2026](https://github.com/maexer1998/MAGICA-School-2026)

### [`groupproject/baltic_sea_sst_copernicusmarine.ipynb`](https://github.com/maexer1998/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/groupproject/baltic_sea_sst_copernicusmarine.ipynb)
**Baltic Sea SST from Copernicus Marine**

Accesses the DMI Baltic Sea L4 SST NRT dataset via `copernicusmarine`. Loads a month of SST data, converts from Kelvin to Celsius, and applies a spatial mask to remove Norwegian/Skagerrak waters and Swedish lakes (Vänern, Vättern) before plotting interactively with `hvplot`.

---

### [`groupproject/baltic_sea_wave_copernicusmarine.ipynb`](https://github.com/maexer1998/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/groupproject/baltic_sea_wave_copernicusmarine.ipynb)
**Baltic Sea Wave Hindcast — Storm Babet (October 2023)**

Examines five wave parameters (significant height, max individual height, wind-sea height, peak period, mean direction) from the CMEMS Baltic Wave Hindcast during Storm Babet (20–21 Oct 2023), which caused 1.5 m surges at Warnemünde. Creates an animated Panel app with wave height raster and `geoviews` vector field arrows showing wave direction.

---

### [`groupproject/baltic_sst_yearly_comparison.ipynb`](https://github.com/maexer1998/MAGICA-School-2026/blob/main/groupproject/baltic_sst_yearly_comparison.ipynb)
**Baltic SST Yearly Comparison**

Computes monthly mean SST from the DMI Baltic SST L4 dataset for multiple years and produces two interactive plots: a spatial map with time slider (one per year) and a seasonal-cycle overlay comparing area-mean SST across years. Uses a Coiled cluster (`eu-central-1`, 30 ARM spot workers) for distributed computation.
